SIMPLE CHOCOLATE CAKE

This recipe is a great recipe to make a simple choclate cake. It uses ingredients that can be found in most kitchen cupboards. You just need to gather all the ingredients, put them into a big bowl and mix them with a blender or mixer! It can also be used to make cupcakes too, if you adjust the cooking times as mentioned below. Then you can decorate your cake however you'd like to. My favourite is Nutella and sliced banana.

Equipment

  • Cake tin or muffin tray
  • Large mixing bowl
  • Electric mixer
  • Cooling rack
  • Fork
  • Weighing scales
  • Smaller bowls, to hold ingredients

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups flour
  • 3/4 cocoa powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Grease and flour your baking pan or line your muffin pan with paper liners for cupcakes.
  3. In a large bowl, combine sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla to dry mixture and mix with an electric mixer on medium for about 2 minutes. Gently mix in boiling water. Note: Your batter will seem thin, it's ok.. it's supposed to!
  5. Pour into pans and bake. Cool on wire racks.
